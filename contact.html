Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our products and services

What is the accuracy of DG®-II?

The DG®-II utilizes a proprietary semiconductor sensor and custom IC design, calibrated in our TAF-accredited lab following ISO 17025 and ISO 6789 standards, ensuring industry-leading accuracy.

What units are supported?

The DG®-II automatically converts between Nm, lbf-ft, lbf-in, and kgf-m, allowing you to work in your preferred measurement system without manual calculations.

Can I get a custom model?

Yes! Stand Tools designs new models according to varying market patterns and serves customers with various combinations. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Does each wrench come with a certificate?

Yes. Each DG®-II digital torque wrench is inspected in our TAF-accredited lab and comes with a certificate report to maintain quality assurance.
