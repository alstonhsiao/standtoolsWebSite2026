DG®-II Digital Torque Wrench

The ultimate precision tool — utilizing semiconductor technology and advanced IC design for exact torque measurement

Precision Redefined

The DG®-II digital torque wrench utilizes the latest semiconductor technology and Taiwan computer techniques to make the super CPU and special sensor, applying to fasten screws precisely. Compared with a traditional torque tool, DG®-II can show the exact applied torque.

This DG®-II has various advantages including showing an accurate value, long-term and easy use, and small power consumption. It is perfect for automobile, motorcycle, aircraft, engines, electronic equipment, and machines.

Key Features

What makes DG®-II the professional's choice

Large LCD Display

A large LCD and a stable torque increment avoid misreading, which is often occurred with mechanical torque wrenches. Clear and precise readout in any lighting condition.

LED Light Indicators

LED lights are flashing when closing the target torque, providing clear visual alert so you never over-tighten or under-tighten fasteners.

Dual-Tone Buzzer

Two tones of a loud buzzer are easily recognized when closing the target torque. The audible alert ensures accuracy even in noisy environments.

Vibration Alert *optional

The square or round handle is vibrating when reaching the target torque. Tactile feedback for situations where visual or audible alerts may not be sufficient.

Automatic Unit Conversion

Unit conversions are automatically made among Nm, lbf-ft, lbf-in, and kgf-m. Switch between units seamlessly without manual calculation.

Memory Storage *optional

The DG-II® digital torque wrench has the capacity to store up to 32 torque values in memory, and can be reviewed easily after working for quality assurance records.

Low Power Consumption

Designed for long-term use with minimal power consumption. Extended battery life ensures the tool is always ready when you need it on the job.

High Accuracy

Using Stand Tools' proprietary sensor and IC design, the DG®-II delivers industry-leading accuracy for all torque measurements.
