DG®-II Digital Torque Wrench

Utilizing the latest semiconductor technology and Taiwan computer techniques, the DG®-II makes a super CPU and special sensor for precision fastening.

Compared with a traditional torque tool, DG®-II can show the exact applied torque with a large LCD and stable torque increment, avoiding misreading common with mechanical torque wrenches.
